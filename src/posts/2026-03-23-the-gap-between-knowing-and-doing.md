title: "The gap between knowing and doing"
date: 2026-03-23
categories: ["Ideas", "Productivity"]
layout: post.njk
---

Everyone knows they should exercise more, eat better, save money, read books, and be kinder.

Knowing isn't the bottleneck. Doing is.

The gap between knowing and doing is where most of our frustration lives. We feel guilty because we *know* what to do but don't do it. And then we seek more knowledge, as if the next book or course will finally bridge the gap.

It won't. The only thing that bridges the gap is action. Imperfect, uncomfortable, sometimes embarrassing action.

Start before you're ready. The doing teaches you things the knowing never will.
