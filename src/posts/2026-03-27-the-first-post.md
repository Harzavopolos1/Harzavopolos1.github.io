Every blog starts somewhere.

This is that somewhere. A blank page that's no longer blank. A decision to show up and share something, even when the voice in your head says *who's going to read this?*

The answer doesn't matter. What matters is the practice. Writing clarifies thinking. Publishing creates accountability. And over time, a body of work builds up — one post at a time.

So here we are. Post number one.

The hardest part is already done.
