title: "Simplicity is a feature"
date: 2026-03-24

---

When someone says "it's too simple," they're usually paying you a compliment without knowing it.

Simple is hard. Simple means you've thought about what to leave out. It means you understand the problem well enough to solve it without unnecessary complexity.

A website that loads instantly, has no distractions, and lets you read — that's not *lacking* features. That's a design choice. A deliberate one.

The same goes for products, processes, and plans. If you can remove something without losing the core value, remove it.

What's left is the feature.
